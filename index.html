Ies Chandracant Patkar Vidyalaya-campus

The school was founded in 1989 and is the result of great vision of Shri. Manikrao Lotlikar and Shri. Padmakar Dhamdhere with the generous donation of land by Chandrakant Patkar Trust. The school started with 800 students of KG to Std. III of both English and Marathi medium. It was conducted in two cottage houses with area.Today our school has a total of 2415 students in secondary section alone and is supported by a total of 72 teaching and non teaching staff.

Slit Area

The Stilt area on the ground floor is very useful. PT(Phycical Traning) periods are conducted during the rainy season. Small school programmes are also conducted here. Children of the afternoon session wait here till the morning session children leave.

Every classroom is provided with an educom system which facililates teaching-learning process.
